The Capital Grill

Restaurant and Lounge

The Capital Grill, a Restaurant and Lounge in Gorakhpur. It not only provides tasty food, but also makes sure to provide enough space for the customers to enjoy freely.
It also makes arrangement and decoration exactly the way you want, for parties and celebrations.
If you are willing to spend a weekend night with your special ones, this is definitely the right place to do so.
Also if you are a hookah lover, they are all set to get your mood lifted with the hookah facilities. They also provide some really amazing soft drinks like mocktails and many more.
From hanging out with friends to celebrating a birthday together, they are up for providing all possible comfort to you. Check out the place if you are yet to discover it.
