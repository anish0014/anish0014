Skillonation

Skillonation is an online education platform that aims to foster curiosity-building with creative approach that bridges the skill gap among young minds.

Our flagship product is SKILLONATION KIDS where we focus upon how to increase tinkering and innovation aptitude among K-12 students.

India's First STEAM based company which not only gives hands-on with STEM , ROBOTICS, CODING but also on ART'S AND CRAFT, ASTRONOMY, PHOTOGRAPHY, 3D PRINTING, DESIGNING and other curiosity-building, STEAM-blended courses.

With our ISA program we focus on up-skilling young undergraduates/graduates with industrial requirements. We ensure them to be Job-ready and fit for the Industry by giving them Job assurance and support up to lock-in time.

We invest in student's growth and our motivation is commitment towards his better future.
