The Six Pm Show

The Six PM Show, a student-centric and student-run firm, working from SRM Institute of Science and Technology. We hold open-mics and live performances, which welcome a talented lot of all experiences, to showcase their virtues. Brimming with entertaining expertise, we make sure that each form of art is given a well-deserved pedestal of appreciation, hence standing by our theme: “Where art meets stage”. We have had an uphill, uniform growth, despite the precarious times, and have been consistent with all our content types, across all of our platforms and predict to stay on the same trajectory of success in the forthcoming years.
